Steps to Activate Prime Video on Your TV

Here are the standard steps to register and activate Prime Video on most smart TVs, Roku, Fire TV, or other streaming devices. If you run into any trouble, our team is available 24/7 at the number below.

  1. Install the Prime Video app from your device's app store or channel store.
  2. Launch the Prime Video application on your TV/device.
  3. Select "Sign in" or the registration option (avoid typing credentials directly on TV if possible).
  4. Choose the option to get an activation/registration code (usually 5-6 characters) displayed on screen.
  5. On a phone, tablet, or computer, visit the official site: amazon.com/code
  6. Sign in to your Amazon account.
  7. Enter the code shown on your TV screen and submit.
  8. Your device should activate within seconds. Enjoy streaming!

Always use the official Amazon website for code entry. We provide phone guidance only and never ask for passwords, payment info, or remote access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why won't my activation code work?
Codes expire quickly (~15-30 min), may have typos, or be for the wrong account. Generate a fresh code and double-check entry.
Prime Video keeps buffering or freezing
Usually caused by slow internet, Wi-Fi interference, outdated app, or device cache. Restart router/device, or call us for more tips.
Is this an official Amazon support line?
No — we are an independent helpline. For official support, visit amazon.com/help or use Prime Video app settings.
Do you charge for support?
Basic phone guidance is free. Some extended/premium sessions may have fees — see our Refund Policy below.
How do I update the Prime Video app?
Go to your device's app store, search for Prime Video, and select Update if available.
